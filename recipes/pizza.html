Oompa Loompa Pizza!

Ingredients

  • Refined wheat flour
  • Diced cheeses
  • Veggies

Directions

  1. Find and capture an oompa loompa.
  2. Make sure the oompa-loompa is tamed by feeding him chocolate.
  3. If it tries to revolt for freedom, whack it.
  4. Keep whacking until oompa-loompa is obedient.
  5. Ask oompa loompa to make pizza.
  6. Success!
